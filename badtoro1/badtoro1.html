BAD TORO is created by Jordi Nogués, a company located in Barcelona and dedicated to the design and manufacture of collections for Duty Free shops in airports around the world. Founded in 1996 by Jordi Nogués, is headquartered in Barcelona, Spain.

BAD TORO a pet created by the design team consisting of Andrés Barzi and Jordi Nogués. The final design came by the spring of 2005.

His defiant look shows both nobility and courage, which in some ways provides a humane attitude a young audience may easily empathize with.
